Seafood Fried Rice

Description

A few weeks ago, I shared my super simple and delivious Chinese-style 10 minute vegetarian fried rice. Now, it's time to switch up the flavours and add in someone protein. 10-minute seafood fried rice is on the menu tonight. It's a one-pan, quick and easy, better than takeout meal that can be made at home tastier and with healthier ingredients. It's the easiest weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • tiger prawns
  • eggs
  • white rice
  • vegetables
  • seasonings
  • satay sauce
  • green onions
  • roasted white sesame seeds (optional)
  • masago caviar (optional)

Steps

  1. ook the prawns over medium-high heat with ginger
  2. Make some space and add in the eggs with a pinch of green onions, and scramble
  3. Stir in carrots, sweet corn and peas (and any other vegetables if using)
  4. Stir in rice
  5. Continuously stir to cook evenly and distribute
  6. Add in seasoning (satay sauce, sesame oil and salt) and continously stir
  7. Add maggi sauce and green onions and stir to combine
  8. Top with a sprinkle of sesame seeds and masago caviar (roe) on top, and serve
